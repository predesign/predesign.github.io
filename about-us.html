宁 宁 / Nìng Níng

 

1998年9月出生于吉林松原，视觉传达设计行业从业者。2020年6月毕业于吉林建筑大学建筑环境与能源应用工程专业，获工学学士学位；2022年7月毕业于西安美术学院视觉传达设计系，获艺术学学士学位。

 

Born in September 1998 in Songyuan, Jilin, I am a practitioner in the visual communication design industry.

Graduated from Jilin Jianzhu University in June 2020 with a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Building Environment and Energy Application Engineering;

Graduated from the Department of Visual Communication Design at Xi'an Academy of Fine Arts in July 2022 with a Bachelor of Arts degree.
